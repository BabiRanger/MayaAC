Question 2

Determine which sinusoid leads and by how much.

$$ v(t) = 10 \cos(4t - 60^\circ) $$ $$ i(t) = 4 \sin(4t + 50^\circ) $$
💡 Hint 1: Convert to Cosine

Convert $i(t)$ to cosine: $\sin(\theta) = \cos(\theta - 90^\circ)$. So $50 - 90 = -40$.

🔑 Show Answer

Answer: $i(t)$ leads $v(t)$ by $20^\circ$.
