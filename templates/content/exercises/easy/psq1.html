Question 1

Given the two voltages below, determine the phase angle between them and state which one lags the other.

$$ v_1 = 20 \sin(\omega t + 60^\circ) $$ $$ v_2 = 60 \cos(\omega t - 10^\circ) $$
💡 Hint 1: Common Format

You cannot compare Sine and Cosine directly. Convert $v_1$ to Cosine using the identity: $\sin(\theta) = \cos(\theta - 90^\circ)$.

🔑 Show Answer

Answer: $v_1$ lags $v_2$ by $20^\circ$.
