💡 Hint 1: Core Formulas

Start by finding the resonant frequency $\omega_o = \frac{1}{\sqrt{LC}}$. Then, find the quality factor using $Q = \frac{\omega_o L}{R}$.

💡 Hint 2: Half-Power Frequencies

Use the exact formulas for a series RLC circuit: $\omega_{1,2} = \mp \frac{R}{2L} + \sqrt{\left(\frac{R}{2L}\right)^2 + \frac{1}{LC}}$. Ensure your units are correct (krad/s vs rad/s).

💡 Hint 3: Power Calculations

Remember that the source voltage is given as a peak value ($V_m = 50\text{V}$). Therefore, average power at resonance is $P = \frac{V_m^2}{2R}$. At the half-power frequencies, the power is exactly half of the resonant power.
