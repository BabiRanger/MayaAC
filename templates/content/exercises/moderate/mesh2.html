💡 Hint 1: Express Reactances as Impedances

Before writing your mesh loops, convert all reactive elements to rectangular complex numbers:
• Inductive Impedance: $Z_1 = +jX_L = +j2\ \Omega$
• Shared Resistive Impedance: $Z_2 = R = 4\ \Omega$
• Capacitive Impedance: $Z_3 = -jX_C = -j1\ \Omega$

💡 Hint 2: Handling Polar Coordinates and Division

When solving the determinants equation for $\mathbf{I}_1$, you'll get a rectangular expression: $\frac{-16 - j2}{2 + j4}$. Do not try to divide rectangular numbers directly! Transform both the numerator and denominator into polar coordinates first ($M \angle \theta$) and subtract the angles.
