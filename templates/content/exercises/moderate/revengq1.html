Question 1

Two elements are connected in series. Find the values of the Resistor (R) and Inductor (L).

Given:

  • Current $i(t) = 12 \cos(2t - 30^\circ)$ A
  • Voltage $v(t) = 180 \cos(2t + 10^\circ)$ V
💡 Hint 1: Total Impedance

Calculate total impedance first using phasors: $Z = \mathbf{V} / \mathbf{I}$.

💡 Hint 2: Rectangular Form

Convert the resulting polar impedance $Z$ into rectangular form $R + jX$. The real part is R, the imaginary part is $\omega L$.

🔑 Show Answer

Answer: $R = 11.49 \Omega$, $L = 4.82$ H
