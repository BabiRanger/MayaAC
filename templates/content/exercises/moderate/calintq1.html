Question 1

Find $v(t)$ in the following equation using the phasor approach:

$$ v(t) + \int v(t)dt = 5 \cos(t + 45^\circ) $$
💡 Hint 1: Transform to Phasor Domain

Remember that integration in the time domain becomes division by $j\omega$ in the phasor domain. Here, $\omega = 1$.

🔑 Show Answer

Answer: $3.536 \cos(t + 90^\circ)$ V
