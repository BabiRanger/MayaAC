Question 1

If $v_s(t) = 5 \cos(2t)$ V, find the output voltage $v_o(t)$ across the inductor.

Figure: Series circuit with R=2Ω, C=0.25F, L=1H

💡 Hint 1: Impedances

Calculate impedances first. $\omega = 2$.
$Z_L = j\omega L = j2$, $Z_C = 1/(j\omega C) = -j2$.

💡 Hint 2: Total Impedance

notice that $Z_L + Z_C = j2 - j2 = 0$. What does this imply about resonance?

🔑 Show Answer

Answer: $v_o(t) = 5 \cos(2t + 90^\circ)$ V
