Summary & Pro-Tips

Mastering nodal analysis requires consistency in your sign conventions.

  • Current Direction: It is often easiest to assume all unknown currents leave the node.
  • Format: $G_{11}V_1 - G_{12}V_2 = I_s$ (where $G$ is conductance $1/R$).

KCL REFRESHER

$$ \sum I_{in} = \sum I_{out} $$

Avoid the "Reference" Trap

Always choose the node with the most connections as your Ground. This simplifies your equations by making more terms zero!
