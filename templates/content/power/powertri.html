The Power Triangle

Similar to the Impedance Triangle, the Power Triangle visualizes the relationship between the three types of power:

  • Real Power (P): Measured in *Watts (W)*. The actual work done.
  • Reactive Power (Q): Measured in *VAR*. Stored energy in L or C.
  • Apparent Power (S): Measured in *VA*. The total power delivered.

Fig 2. Geometrical relationship: $S = \sqrt{P^2 + Q^2}$

$$ PF = \cos(\theta) = \frac{P}{S} $$
