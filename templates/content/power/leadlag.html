Leading vs. Lagging PF

The "nature" of the power factor depends on whether the load is more Inductive or Capacitive.

  • Lagging (Inductive): Current lags voltage. Common in motors and transformers.
  • Leading (Capacitive): Current leads voltage. Common in synchronous motors or capacitor banks.

REMEMBER CIVIL

In an Inductor (L), Voltage leads Current (I), so the Power Factor Lags.
