Passive Element

A resistor is a classic passive element. Let's apply the sign convention rules to it.

  • Voltage Polarity: There is a drop from (+) to (-).
  • Current Direction: The current $i$ enters the (+) terminal.
  • The Rule: Since current enters positive, we use:
    $$ p = +vi $$
  • Conclusion: Since $p > 0$, the resistor absorbs power and dissipates it as heat.
