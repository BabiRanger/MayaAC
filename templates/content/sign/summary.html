Summary & Key Takeaways

The Passive Sign Convention is the universal rule for determining power flow. It applies to all circuit elements, including Resistors, Inductors, Capacitors, and Sources.

Current Direction Formula Conclusion
Current enters (+) Terminal
(Standard for Passive Elements) 		$$ p = +vi $$ Absorbs Power
(Consumes Energy)
Current enters (-) Terminal
(Standard for Active Elements) 		$$ p = -vi $$ Supplies Power
(Delivers Energy)

Pro Tip: Avoid Sign Errors

Always draw your current arrows and label your voltage polarity (+/-) clearly on your diagram before starting calculations. If you skip this step, you have a 50% chance of getting the sign wrong!
