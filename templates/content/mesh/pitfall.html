Summary & Pro-Tips

Mesh analysis is often simpler than Nodal analysis when a circuit has many parallel branches but few loops.

  • Mutual Resistance: Remember that components shared by two meshes will have two currents flowing through them (e.g., $I_1 - I_2$).
  • Matrix Format: $R_{11}I_1 - R_{12}I_2 = V_s$.

KVL SIGN RULE

When moving in the direction of the current, a resistor always creates a voltage drop ($-IR$).

The "Clockwise" Secret

If you assign all mesh currents in the same direction (usually clockwise), the shared resistor terms in your equations will always be negative. This makes checking your work much easier!
