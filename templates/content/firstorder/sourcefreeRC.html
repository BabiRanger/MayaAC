2. The Source-Free RC Circuit

When a DC source is suddenly disconnected, the energy stored in the capacitor is released into the resistor. By applying Kirchhoff's Current Law (KCL), we find that the voltage decays exponentially.

  • The Voltage Response: The voltage across the capacitor at any time $t$ is given by:
    $$ v(t) = V_0 e^{-t/\tau} $$
  • Time Constant ($\tau$): Determines how fast the voltage decays. For an RC circuit, it is the product of equivalent resistance and capacitance:
    $$ \tau = R_{eq}C $$
