Y-Parameters (Short-Circuit)

Y-parameters (Admittance parameters) are the exact opposite of Z-parameters. They express port currents in terms of port voltages.

  • The Equations:
    $$ I_1 = \mathbf{y}_{11}V_1 + \mathbf{y}_{12}V_2 $$ $$ I_2 = \mathbf{y}_{21}V_1 + \mathbf{y}_{22}V_2 $$
  • How to find them: We short-circuit one of the ports (voltage = 0) to solve.
  • Example: To find $y_{21}$, we short Port 2 so $V_2 = 0$. Then, $\mathbf{y}_{21} = \frac{I_2}{V_1}$.
