Z-Parameters (Open-Circuit)

Z-parameters (Impedance parameters) express the port voltages ($V_1, V_2$) in terms of the port currents ($I_1, I_2$).

  • The Equations:
    $$ V_1 = \mathbf{z}_{11}I_1 + \mathbf{z}_{12}I_2 $$ $$ V_2 = \mathbf{z}_{21}I_1 + \mathbf{z}_{22}I_2 $$
  • How to find them: We leave one port open-circuited (current = 0) to solve for the parameters.
  • Example: To find $z_{11}$, we open Port 2 so $I_2 = 0$. Then, $\mathbf{z}_{11} = \frac{V_1}{I_1}$.

Matrix Form

$$ \begin{bmatrix} V_1 \\ V_2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{z}_{11} & \mathbf{z}_{12} \\ \mathbf{z}_{21} & \mathbf{z}_{22} \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} I_1 \\ I_2 \end{bmatrix} $$

Measured in Ohms ($\Omega$)
