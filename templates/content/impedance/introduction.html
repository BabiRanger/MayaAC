Introduction to Impedance (Z)

In AC circuits, opposition to current comes from more than just resistors. Impedance is the total opposition offered by a circuit to the flow of alternating current.

  • Components: It combines Resistance ($R$) and Reactance ($X$).
  • Unit: Measured in Ohms ($\Omega$).
  • Mathematical Form: Expressed as a complex number:
    $$ \mathbf{Z} = R + jX $$
