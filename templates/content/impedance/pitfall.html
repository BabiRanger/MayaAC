Common Mistakes

When solving AC circuits, students often make errors in sign convention and units.

  • The "j" Sign: Forgetting that $X_C$ is negative ($-j$) in rectangular form.
  • Frequency Units: Mixing up $f$ (Hz) and $\omega$ (rad/s).
  • The Tip:
    $\omega = 2\pi f$

MISTAKE ALERT

Never simply add $R$ and $X$ algebraically (e.g., $3\Omega + 4\Omega \neq 7\Omega$). Use the Pythagorean theorem!
