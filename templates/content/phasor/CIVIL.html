The CIVIL Mnemonic

To remember which quantity leads or lags, we use the word CIVIL.

  • C - I - V : In a Capacitor, I (current) comes before V (voltage).
  • V - I - L : V (voltage) comes before I (current) in an Inductor (L).
C I V I L

A student favorite for exams!

Pro Tip: When you see the word "Before" in AC, it means "Leads." So in an inductor, Voltage leads Current.
