Phasors: Pure Capacitor

In a pure capacitor, the current leads the voltage by exactly $90^\circ$.

  • Voltage Equation: $\mathbf{V}_C = \mathbf{I} (-jX_C)$.
  • The -j Effect: The $-j$ operator shifts the voltage vector $90^\circ$ clockwise:
    $$ \mathbf{V}_C = | I X_C | \angle (\theta - 90^\circ) $$
  • Current: Current is said to lead the voltage.
